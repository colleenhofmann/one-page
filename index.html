Colleen Hofmann is an App Deployment Director living in Lancaster, Pennsylvania

Early in life, I fell in love with creating things: art, words, websites. You name it. My youth was spent tinkering with HTML and CSS, tweaking friends' web pages and journaling.

Today, I am the Deployment Director at Workhorse Development, an app dev shop, where we create solutions for hard-working B2B companies in construction, chemical, agriculture and other manufacturing industries.

I'm an advocate for open source, passionate about writing, lover of travel, admirer of native plants, toaster of IPAs and the like, fan of thoughtful conversations and obsessed with approaching problems with creative solutions.

Follow my adventures as I go, and say hello.
